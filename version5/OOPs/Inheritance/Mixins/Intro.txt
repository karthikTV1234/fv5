What are Mixins?
1. Mixins are a way to reuse code across multiple classes without using inheritance.
2. They let you add functionality to classes without creating parent-child relationships.


Why Use Mixins?
  Dart does not support multiple inheritance, but mixins offer a similar mechanism.
